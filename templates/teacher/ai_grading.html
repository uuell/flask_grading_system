How It Works

1
Filter by semester and year (optional) Use the filters above to narrow the class list to the current semester so it's easier to find the right class.
2
Select a class and test This scopes all uploads to the correct students and grade records.
3
Drag & drop the paper images Upload the whole batch at once — JPG or PNG, as many as needed.
4
The AI reads each paper YOLO detects name, score, and label regions. OCR reads the text. Fuzzy matching compares the name against your enrolled students.
5
Confirm or correct each assignment Green = high confidence. Yellow = uncertain. Red = unreadable (manual pick required).
6
Grades are written automatically Once confirmed, the OCR score is saved and the term grade recalculates.
