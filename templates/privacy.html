Privacy Policy

This policy explains what data Acadify collects, how it is used, and how it is protected within the system.

📅 Last updated: January 2026

Overview

Acadify ("the System") is a school grading and student monitoring platform operated by and for educational institutions. This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the system — administrators, teachers, and students.

By using Acadify, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use and contact your institution's administrator.

Data We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to operate the system:

  • Account data: Email address, hashed password, and user role.
  • Student profile: Student number, full name, department, program, year level, and section.
  • Teacher profile: Employee number, full name, department, and specialization.
  • Academic data: Class enrollments, test records, component scores, and calculated grades.
  • Uploaded files: Paper images uploaded through the AI Grading feature (stored temporarily for OCR processing).
  • Activity metadata: Timestamps for grade submissions and account creation dates.
We do not collect: payment information, precise location data, biometric data, or any data not directly related to academic record-keeping.

How We Use It

All data collected is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authenticating users and managing access by role.
  • Displaying grade records to the appropriate student or teacher.
  • Computing GPA, term grades, and academic standing.
  • Generating reports and CSV exports for institutional records.
  • Running the AI grading pipeline to match uploaded paper images to enrolled students.

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any commercial purpose.

Storage & Security

All data is stored in a secured relational database. Passwords are never stored in plain text — they are hashed using BCrypt before being saved.

  • Database access is restricted to the application server only.
  • Session cookies are HTTP-only and protected from cross-site scripting.
  • Paper images uploaded for AI grading are stored on the server and associated with the specific test record. They are not publicly accessible.
  • Production deployments use HTTPS to encrypt data in transit.

Sharing

Acadify does not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

Data may only be disclosed in the following limited circumstances:

  • To your institution's administrators who have been granted admin access within the system.
  • If required by Philippine law or a valid legal order.
  • To prevent fraud, abuse, or safety threats to the system or its users.

Your Rights

Under the Philippines' Data Privacy Act of 2012 (Republic Act 10173), you have the right to:

  • Be informed about what personal data is being collected and how it is used.
  • Access your personal data held in the system.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request erasure of your data, subject to institutional record-keeping obligations.
  • Object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.

To exercise any of these rights, contact your institution's registrar or the system administrator.

Cookies

Acadify uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. This cookie:

  • Is HTTP-only (not accessible via JavaScript).
  • Does not track browsing activity outside the system.
  • Expires after 7 days of inactivity or upon logout.

No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies are used.

Policy Changes

We may update this policy as the system evolves. When changes are made, the "Last updated" date at the top of this page will be revised. Continued use of the system after an update constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
