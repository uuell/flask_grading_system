About Acadify

Built for educators,
designed for results

Acadify is a modern school grading and student monitoring system built to give teachers, students, and administrators a clear, real-time view of academic performance.

01 Our Mission

We believe that grading should never be a bottleneck. Our mission is to eliminate the paperwork, the spreadsheets, and the guesswork — replacing them with a fast, accurate, and transparent system that puts student outcomes first. Acadify was built from the ground up for Philippine higher education institutions, following the standard PH grading scale and semester structure.

Real-Time Grades

Students see their grades the moment a teacher submits them. No waiting, no confusion.

AI Auto-Grading

Upload paper images and let the AI pipeline extract names, scores, and match them to enrolled students.

Role-Based Access

Admins, teachers, and students each see exactly what they need — nothing more, nothing less.

GPA Calculator

Students can estimate their GPA using a built-in tool pre-loaded with their current subjects.

CSV Export

Download full grade sheets in Excel or CSV format for official records and reporting.

Secure by Design

Passwords are hashed using BCrypt. Data is scoped strictly by role — no cross-account access.

02 Who It's For

Acadify is designed for colleges and universities in the Philippines — particularly institutions running on a semestral calendar with the standard 1.0–5.0 grading system. Whether you're a department with 5 teachers or a college with 500, the system scales to fit without configuration overhead.

03 The Team

Acadify is developed by a team of students that is focused on solving real problems in Philippine tertiary education. We are students, educators, and engineers who understand the friction that comes with manual grade management.

University of Rizal System — Morong This system was built in close collaboration with faculty and students of URS Morong to ensure it reflects actual classroom workflows, not abstract software assumptions.
